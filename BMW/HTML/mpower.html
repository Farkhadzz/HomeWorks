BMW M5


With a fuel consumption of 14.8 litres/100km - 19 mpg UK - 16 mpg US (Average), 0 to 100 km/h (62mph) in 4.7 seconds, a maximum top speed of 155 mph (250 km/h), a curb weight of 3814 lbs (1730 kgs), the E60 5 Series M5 has a naturally-aspirated V 10 cylinder engine, Petrol motor.This engine produces a maximum power of 507 PS (500 bhp - 373 kW) at 7750 rpm and a maximum torque of 520 Nm (383 lb.ft) at 6100 rpm. The power is transmitted to the road by the Rear wheel drive (RWD) with a 7 speed Sequential gearbox.
